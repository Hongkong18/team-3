Our mission and vision


1. To ensure everyone in Hong Kong living with a mental health problem has the recognition, support and respect they need to make the best recovery possible.


2. To provide partnership, collaboration, training, innovation and best practice to facilitate the work of all those involved in improving mental health in Hong Kong.


3. To lead, promote and support the destigmatisation and transformation of community mental health care so that Hong Kong can become a global leader in the field and a model for other Asian cities.


4. To research and audit these strategies and share them internationally.
